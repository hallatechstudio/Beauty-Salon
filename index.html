Our Story

A Passion for Perfection

Brushes & Clippers Salon & Spa is Lagos' destination for premium beauty services. With two thriving branches in Gbagada and Yaba, we've built a community of clients who trust us to bring their beauty visions to life.

Our team of skilled stylists, makeup artists, and spa therapists bring artistry and care to every appointment — whether you're stepping in for a quick trim or a full transformation.
